A hummus without tahini

An easy, budget-friendly version which won't have you missing out; silky smooth.
Here is what you'll need and how to make them:

Ingredients

  • 400g can chickpeas, drained (liquid reserved)
  • 1/2 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Directions

  1. Tip the chickpeas, garlic, lemon juice and olive oil into a small blender or food processor. Season and add the reserved liquid from the can of chickpeas.
  2. Blitz together until smooth, adding more of the reserved chickpea liquid to make a smoother hummus, if you like. Tip the hummus into a bowl, drizzle with more olive oil and chill until ready to serve.
